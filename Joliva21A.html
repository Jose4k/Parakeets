Why are some parakeets blue and others green?

Blue parakeets are mutants!

For pet parakeets, blue is a very common color. This color is a mutation caused by the absence of yellow coloration from the original green. While blue is a safe color for pet birds, a blue parakeet would be easily spotted by predators and have a survival disadvantage in the wild.
